# Snapshot 001: Foundation

The foundation of ODPM is Decision Readiness.

Decision Readiness means a team can see the current state of a decision:

- the goal,
- the knowns,
- the unknowns,
- the relevant constraints,
- the open disagreements,
- the likely consequences,
- and the next step.

When this state is visible, teams can move with less theater and more intent.
