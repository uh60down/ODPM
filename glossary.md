# Glossary

## Decision Readiness

The degree to which a team can make a responsible decision with visible context, evidence, options, constraints, risks, and consequences.

## PRD Ontology

A model of the concepts and relationships that make a product requirements document useful as a decision record.

## Shared Understanding

Alignment that can be carried across people, meetings, and time without losing the important parts of the decision.

## Snapshot

A numbered capture of the manuscript's thinking at a point in time.
